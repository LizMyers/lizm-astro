## The reframe

Most language apps treat vocabulary as the unit. LingoWise treats *conversation* as the unit. Every word you learn arrives because you needed it in a sentence — not because a deck told you to learn it.

## What it does

- An AI partner that adapts to your level and interests
- Vocabulary cards auto-generated from chats, grouped by topic
- Scenario practice — market, bakery, immigration desk — with native pronunciation
- Spaced-repetition quizzes, offline mode
- PDF import and cross-device sync on the Pro tier

## Aesthetic

Travel-forward. Warm imagery — airplane-window views, market stalls, fruit on a table — paired with dual-language chat bubbles as the hero motif. Friendly and lifestyle-oriented, not corporate ed-tech.
